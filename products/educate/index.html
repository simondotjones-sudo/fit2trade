Common questions

Questions about Educate

How quickly can we go live? +

Most customers are live within 3–5 business days. We set up your branded portal, load your org structure, assign your first course library and give your admin team a walkthrough. Your employees can start learning the same week.

Do employees need to download an app? +

No — Educate runs in any modern browser on any device. Employees click a link, log in with one click and start learning. Nothing to install, nothing to update. This dramatically increases completion rates compared to app-based systems.

Can we use our own course content? +

Yes. Educate supports SCORM, AICC and xAPI — so you can import existing content from any authoring tool. We also offer our ENHANCE service to build bespoke courses for your brand, products or internal procedures if you want something purpose-built.

How are certifications handled when they expire? +

Certifications are set with expiry dates that match regulatory requirements (e.g. Manual Handling typically every 3 years). Before a certificate expires, both the employee and their manager receive automated reminders. The employee is then automatically assigned the refresher course.

We have multiple sites — can managers only see their own teams? +

Yes. The permission structure in Educate mirrors your org chart. Site managers see only their site's data. Regional managers see their region. Head office sees everything. You control who can assign content, run reports and manage users at each level.

Does Educate integrate with our HR or payroll system? +

Educate is designed to be standalone — zero IT setup required. For larger organisations that want to sync user data with HR systems, we support CSV imports and our team can discuss API integration options during your demo.
