Common questions

Questions about Operate

How is Operate different from Ensure?+

Ensure manages Health & Safety compliance — incidents, risk assessments, safety statements. Operate manages operational performance — brand standards, loss prevention, visit reporting. They're complementary; many customers use both. Ensure is compliance-focused, Operate is operations-focused.

Can brand partners and franchisors see results?+

Yes — the permission structure supports franchisor access. Brand partners can be given a view of their franchisees' completion data and Brand Audit scores without being able to edit or configure anything. This makes it easy to share evidence of compliance with brand partners proactively.

Do area managers need to be at the site to complete a visit report?+

No — but for physical site visits, the app captures everything in real time during the visit so there's nothing to write up afterwards. For remote management audits, managers can conduct assessments via the app and raise actions for Site Leaders to complete, all without travelling to the location.

Can we customise checklists to match our brand standards?+

Absolutely. Operate is configured around your specific brand standards and operational requirements during setup. Our team works with you to build the exact checklists your brand auditors use — so your digital and physical audit processes are identical.

How quickly can we get started?+

Most customers are live within one to two weeks. The setup involves configuring your site structure, building your checklists and giving your team access. Our onboarding team manages this for you — there's nothing for your IT team to do.
