Common questions

Questions about Enclose

What types of licences can Enclose manage?+

Enclose is designed for any organisational licence — fire safety certificates, insurance documents, planning permissions, liquor licences, environmental permits, food business registrations, and more. If it has an expiry date and an authority issues it, Enclose can manage it.

Who can upload licences — can site managers do it?+

No — only Organisation Administrators can upload licences. This is a deliberate design decision to ensure data integrity. Site-level users can retrieve and view their assigned licences, but cannot upload, replace or delete them. This protects against accidental or unauthorised changes.

What happens when we upload a replacement licence?+

Enclose automatically archives the previous version and replaces it with the new upload. Your archive is retained securely and remains accessible to Safety Administrators for audit purposes. Nothing is permanently deleted.

Can we have different licence requirements for different sites?+

Yes. You can set minimum licence requirements that apply across your entire portfolio, or configure requirements individually per site. This is useful when some locations have additional licences (e.g. late-night licences, outdoor seating permissions) that don't apply to all sites.

How does Enclose fit alongside Ensure?+

They're complementary. Ensure manages your operational H&S compliance — routines, incidents, risk assessments. Enclose manages your organisational licences — the certificates and permits issued by authorities. Together they give you a complete compliance picture in one platform.
