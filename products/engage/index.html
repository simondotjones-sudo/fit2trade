Common questions

Questions about Engage

Do employees need to download an app?+

No. Engage works in any modern browser on any smartphone, tablet or desktop — no app download needed. Employees access it via a simple link or bookmark. This is important for frontline workers who may be reluctant to install a company app on their personal phone.

How do employees log in without a company email?+

Employees can log in with their personal email address, a staff ID number, or via a one-click magic link sent by their manager. Admins control the login method that makes most sense for their workforce. No company email address is required at any point.

Can we target communications to specific teams or sites?+

Yes — content can be published to all employees, or targeted to specific sites, departments, roles or seniority levels. A head office announcement can go to everyone; a site-specific policy update can go only to that location. Employees only see content relevant to them.

How does Engage connect to Educate and Elevate?+

All three products share the same login within the Fit2Trade platform. From Engage, employees can navigate directly to their training courses (Educate) and their wellbeing resources (Elevate) without logging in again. For employees, it feels like one seamless app. For administrators, it's managed from one portal.

Can we use Engage even if we don't have other Fit2Trade products?+

Yes — Engage can be purchased and deployed as a standalone product. However, most customers find the most value when it's part of the wider platform alongside Educate and Elevate, since employees naturally move between communications, training and wellbeing in a single session.
