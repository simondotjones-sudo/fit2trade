Common questions

Questions about Ensure

How long does it take to get up and running? +

Most customers are live within 3–5 business days. Our onboarding team configures your account, pre-loads your industry templates and walks your admin through the platform. There's no lengthy IT project — if you have an internet connection, you're ready.

Does it work on mobile? +

Yes — Ensure is designed for frontline workers first. Staff can complete routines, report incidents, log hazards and access safety statements from any smartphone or tablet. No app download required; it works in the browser.

Can I use Ensure across multiple sites? +

Absolutely — Ensure is built for multi-site operations. You get a single dashboard showing compliance across every location, with the ability to drill down by site, region or individual check type. Pricing scales per location.

Does Ensure satisfy HSA and HIQA requirements? +

Yes. Ensure's Safety Statement module, Incident Reporting and Audit tools are designed to satisfy Irish and UK regulatory requirements including HSA (Republic of Ireland), HSE (UK/Northern Ireland) and HIQA (healthcare). Our team are compliance specialists and stay on top of regulatory changes.

What happens to our existing paper records? +

You keep your paper records for whatever retention period applies to your industry. From go-live, all new records are captured digitally in Ensure. Most customers run a brief parallel period, then phase out paper entirely within 30–60 days.

Is our data secure and GDPR compliant? +

Yes. Fit2Trade is GDPR compliant, with data hosted on EU servers. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We maintain a full Trust Portal with our security policies, penetration testing results and DPA documentation available on request.
