Common questions

Questions about Elevate

Can managers see which employees used which modules?+

No. Individual activity is completely private. Managers see aggregate engagement data — for example, what percentage of employees accessed the platform this month — but never which specific person accessed which specific content. This is fundamental to the programme's design.

Is employee participation mandatory?+

No — participation is entirely voluntary. Elevate is not a compliance module and there are no completion requirements. Employees access what's relevant to them, when they want, at their own pace. This is deliberate: mandatory wellbeing programmes almost always fail because the coercion undermines trust.

How does Elevate differ from an EAP (Employee Assistance Programme)?+

Traditional EAPs are reactive — they provide counselling after an employee is in crisis. Elevate is proactive — it builds resilience, awareness and coping skills before problems escalate. The two are complementary; Elevate significantly reduces the load on reactive EAP services by addressing root causes earlier.

Can we add our own content or brand the portal?+

Yes. The Elevate portal is branded to your organisation — your logo, colours and a custom welcome message. You can also add internal links and resources. If you want to create bespoke wellbeing content specific to your workplace, our ENHANCE custom course creation service can build that for you.

Does Elevate work alongside Educate?+

Yes — they sit within the same platform and employees access both through a single login. Educate handles compliance and professional development (mandatory). Elevate handles personal wellbeing (voluntary). Together they give you a complete employee development and support offering.
