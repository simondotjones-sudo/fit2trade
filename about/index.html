Our Story

Built for the frontline.

We started Fit2Trade because managing compliance in a busy workplace was harder than it needed to be. We fixed that.

Who we are

One system. One login.
Zero complexity.

Fit2Trade was founded with a simple belief: that compliance shouldn't be a burden on the people doing the real work. Frontline managers were drowning in paperwork, checklists on clipboards, and half a dozen different systems that never talked to each other.

We built a single, integrated platform that brings H&S management, operational checklists, compliance training, employee wellbeing, and licence tracking into one place — accessible from any device, anywhere.

Today, Fit2Trade is trusted by hundreds of businesses across Ireland and the UK — from independent retailers and credit unions to hotel groups and healthcare providers. Our mission remains the same: make every workplace safer, more compliant, and simpler to run.

What drives us

Our values

Everything we build, say, and do comes back to these principles.

🎯
Simplicity first

We obsess over making complex compliance requirements simple enough for anyone to handle — without a manual or a training day.

🤝
Customer obsession

Our Customer Experience team exists for one reason: to make sure every single client gets results. We measure ourselves by your outcomes, not our metrics.

🔒
Trust and transparency

We're honest about what our platform does and doesn't do, what regulations require, and what you need to do to stay compliant. No jargon. No hidden extras.

⚡
Always improving

Regulation changes. Industries evolve. AI opens up new possibilities. We ship continuous improvements so your platform grows with you — not against you.

🌍
Real-world expertise

Our safety, content, and customer teams come from the industries we serve. We understand what a busy Friday night in a restaurant looks like — because we've been there.

🤖
AI for good

We use AI to remove drudge work from compliance — automated risk assessments, predictive alerts, smart reporting — so your people can focus on the work that matters.
