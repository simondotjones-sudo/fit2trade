Common questions

Questions from retail managers

Does Fit2Trade cover both the Republic of Ireland and the UK?+

Yes — Fit2Trade is used by retailers across the Republic of Ireland, Northern Ireland and Great Britain. Our safety content, courses and templates are aligned to both the Safety, Health and Welfare at Work Act 2005 (ROI) and the Health and Safety at Work Act 1974 (UK). Your dashboard indicates which regulatory framework applies to each location.

What if an HSA inspector arrives and I can't get a signal?+

Your compliance dashboard can be accessed from any internet-connected device. For critical documents like your Safety Statement, these can also be printed or saved locally as PDFs before an inspection. In practice, our customers find that a standard mobile data connection is always sufficient — the portal is optimised for mobile use.

How quickly can we be set up across multiple stores?+

Most retail customers with 5–20 stores are fully live within 5–7 business days. We set up your store structure, load your safety templates and train your admin team. Individual stores are typically onboarded same-day — staff can start completing routines and training immediately.

Can we customise the safety checklists to our specific store layout?+

Absolutely — every checklist is fully customisable. We provide retail-specific templates as a starting point (daily opening checks, fire exit inspection, refrigeration temperature logs, etc.) and your team can add, remove or modify any check to match your specific operations.

Does the system work for stores with a food-to-go or deli operation?+

Yes — Ensure includes a dedicated food safety management module alongside the H&S system. This covers HACCP digital records, temperature logs, allergen management, cleaning schedules and food handler training records. Everything an EHO or FSAI inspector would ask to see from a food operation.
