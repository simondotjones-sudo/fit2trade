Common questions

Questions from early years providers

I'm a sole trader childminder with just a few staff — is Fit2Trade suitable for me?+

Yes — Fit2Trade is used by settings from single-person childminders to large creche networks. For small settings, the platform takes the paper burden away without adding complexity. Setup is simple, and pricing scales with your team size so you're not paying for features you don't need.

Does Fit2Trade cover the Child Care Regulations 2016 (Tusla)?+

Yes — our templates, safety statement structures and course library are built around the Child Care (Pre-School Services) Regulations 2016 in Ireland. For UK settings, we align to the EYFS Statutory Framework (England), the National Care Standards (Scotland) and CIW standards (Wales). Our compliance specialists review these regularly as regulations are updated.

Can I track Garda Vetting / DBS records alongside training records?+

Fit2Trade tracks training certifications and compliance records. While Garda Vetting (NVB) and DBS checks are issued by external bodies, you can upload and store copies of vetting certificates in each staff member's profile alongside their training records — giving you one complete staff compliance record for any inspection.

What if I have part-time or relief staff who work irregular hours?+

Part-time and relief staff are added to the system the same way as full-time staff. They're assigned their required training and their certification status is tracked identically. Tusla inspectors will ask about all staff who work in your setting — Fit2Trade makes sure none of them fall through the cracks regardless of how often they work.
