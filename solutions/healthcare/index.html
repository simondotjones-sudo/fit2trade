Common questions

Questions from care home managers

Does Fit2Trade specifically cover the HIQA National Standards for Residential Care Settings?+

Yes — Fit2Trade's training courses, safety templates and compliance management tools are aligned to the National Standards for Residential Care Settings for Older People (HIQA, 2016) and the associated regulations under S.I. 415/2013. Our healthcare compliance team reviews these standards annually and updates the platform accordingly. CQC customers use the same platform aligned to the Fundamental Standards under the Health and Social Care Act 2008 (Regulated Activities) Regulations 2014.

How does Fit2Trade handle notifiable events to HIQA?+

When an incident is logged in Fit2Trade and classified as a notifiable event (e.g., unexplained death, serious injury, allegation of abuse), the system triggers a mandatory notification workflow. The PIC is alerted immediately and a notification summary — in the format required by HIQA — is generated automatically. The notification is then tracked to completion so nothing falls through.

Can we track patient handling recertification on a 3-year cycle?+

Yes — certification expiry periods are configurable per training area. Manual/patient handling is typically set to a 3-year recertification cycle, while areas like infection prevention and fire safety are set to annual. The system alerts both the staff member and their manager 30 days before any certification expires and automatically assigns the refresher course.

We have agency and bank staff — can they be included in the training matrix?+

Yes — HIQA inspectors will ask about all staff who work in your centre, regardless of employment type. Fit2Trade includes agency and bank staff in the training matrix. They're added to the system before their first shift and assigned the same mandatory training as permanent staff. Their records are retained in your system even after their contract ends.

Can Fit2Trade help us address a HIQA non-compliance finding?+

Absolutely. If you've received a non-compliance finding, Fit2Trade can accelerate your corrective action plan. We can set up your system rapidly, help you get all outstanding training completed, and generate documented evidence for HIQA within your response period. Many customers come to us specifically after a difficult inspection — and leave their follow-up inspection in a much stronger position.
