Common questions

Questions from public sector managers

Can Fit2Trade produce evidence for a DPC investigation or FOI request?+

Yes — your compliance dashboard can produce a complete, timestamped report showing which employees have completed GDPR training, when they completed it, and when any policies were acknowledged. This is exactly the type of evidence the Data Protection Commission looks for when investigating whether an organisation took appropriate staff training measures. The same report format works for internal audit, CAG reviews and FOI-related compliance queries.

Do we need separate training for office staff vs outdoor/maintenance teams?+

Yes — and Fit2Trade manages this automatically. Training requirements are assigned by role and location. Office staff are assigned DSE, manual handling and governance training. Outdoor and maintenance workers are assigned manual handling, fire safety, lone working procedures and any relevant site-specific H&S training. Managers see completion across all groups in one dashboard.

Is the Protected Disclosures training aligned to the 2022 Act amendments?+

Yes — our Protected Disclosures course reflects the Protected Disclosures (Amendment) Act 2022 which significantly expanded the scope of the 2014 legislation. The course covers the updated definitions of “worker” and “wrongdoing”, the new obligations on public bodies, the designated disclosure channels requirement and the enhanced protections against penalisation.

How does Fit2Trade handle seasonal and contract staff?+

Seasonal and contract staff are added to the system with their specific role and training requirements before they start. Their training completion is tracked identically to permanent staff — and their records are retained after their contract ends. For councils and agencies with significant seasonal workforce needs (outdoor maintenance, tourism, events), this ensures compliance records are maintained regardless of workforce fluctuations.

Can we use Fit2Trade to demonstrate compliance during a CAG or internal audit?+

Yes — the one-click compliance report from Fit2Trade provides auditors with a complete view of training records, H&S documentation, risk assessment registers, incident logs and policy acknowledgements. The date-stamped audit trail shows not just that you have policies in place, but that staff were trained on them — which is what a CAG or internal audit review is typically seeking.
